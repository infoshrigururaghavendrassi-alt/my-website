About Us

Rooted in Quality,
Growing with Care

No.1 Agro Clean provides reliable job work services for cleaning and processing all types of seeds and grains. We use advanced machines — destoner, cleaner, and grading machines — to remove dust, stones, impurities, and unwanted particles.

Our services support farmers, traders, wholesalers, and small businesses by helping them get cleaner, better-quality, and market-ready goods. We accept both small and bulk quantity work.

What We Do

Our Job Work Services

Complete cleaning and processing support for all types of seeds and grains.

🌾

Seed Cleaning

We clean different types of seeds by removing dust, stones, mud particles, damaged particles, and unwanted impurities for better market presentation.

🌽

Grain Cleaning

We provide grain cleaning services to improve the quality, appearance, and market value of grains — removing all unwanted particles.

⚙️

Destoning

Our destoner machine removes stones and heavier particles from seeds and grains, improving quality and helping make goods market-ready.

📊

Grading

We grade seeds and grains based on size and quality, giving a more uniform, better-looking final output that buyers trust more.

🏭

Processing & Packing

We process seeds and grains as per customer requirements, focusing on quality, better appearance, and market-ready finishing — with packing support.

📦

Small Batch Job Work

One of our key strengths — we accept small quantity and small batch work, unlike many units that require large minimums. Every farmer matters.

Our Story

A Business Built on
Hard Work & Faith

The journey of No.1 Agro Clean is the story of hard work, dedication, and faith in Shri Guru Raghavendra Swamy.

09
2009 — The Beginning

Smt. Prabha Uppin began her entrepreneurial journey with home-based businesses — tailoring, saree design, a small flour mill, kirana store, and candle making.

🌱
Years of Growth

Each business taught discipline, patience, customer service, and the value of honest work. Her husband Shri Manjunath Uppin supported every step of the way.

With years of dedication and family support, No.1 Agro Clean was launched at APMC Yard, Amargol, Hubli — helping farmers, traders, and wholesalers improve grain quality.
